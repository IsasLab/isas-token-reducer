The Orion billing service exposes a single POST endpoint, `/v1/charge`, which accepts an amount in minor units and an idempotency key. Requests are authenticated with an HMAC-SHA256 signature over the raw body; the shared secret rotates every 90 days and both the current and previous secret are accepted during a 24-hour overlap.

A charge above 500000 minor units requires a second approver named in the `reviewed_by` field, and the ledger records the approver, the timestamp, and the resulting balance. The service guarantees at-most-once execution per idempotency key for 7 days, after which the key expires and a replay is treated as a new charge.

> A 5xx response never confirms a charge; clients must poll `/v1/charge/{id}` before retrying.

Failed settlements are retried on a schedule of 1, 5, 15, and 60 minutes, then parked for manual review by the payments on-call, currently Dana Whitfield.
