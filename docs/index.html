How it works

Three steps to a leaner context

01

Point it at your text or task

Hand ISAS the document, prompt, or job you're about to send to Claude.

02

It trims the redundancy

Strips duplicates, filler, and whitespace — or routes large tasks through condensing subagents.

03

Feed the leaner context to Claude

You send fewer, denser tokens — the model spends its fuel on reasoning, not repetition.

Real savings depend on how redundant your input is — the tool shows you the exact before/after with --stats. No magic numbers.
